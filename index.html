What Oblongix Advisory does

We help organisations capture real value from AI by treating it as an operating model and decision redesign, then delivering the foundations, workflows, and governance required to scale.

Our delivery approach

A structured method that starts with value and leadership alignment, and ends with AI operating safely in production.

  1. Align - clarify value, sponsorship, and scope
  2. Diagnose - assess challenge, constraints, risks, and readiness
  3. Design - define change and roadmap
  4. Build - deliver change and core capabilities
  5. Embed - drive adoption in real business workflows
  6. Govern and Operate - monitor performance, controls, and scale
When we are most useful
  • - You have pilots but few measurable outcomes
  • - Value is unclear or governance is weak
  • - Data ownership and operating constraints block scale
  • - Leaders need a board-ready plan for controls and risk
How we work
  • - Short, focused engagements with clear outputs: diagnostics, decision maps, operating model changes, and delivery support.
  • - Longer term partnerships for ongoing transformation and governance.
Who we work with

Our clients include large corporations, private equity firms, and small to mid-sized businesses across multiple sectors. We also advise government departments.

Typical client contacts include CEOs, CIOs, COOs, and boards who need to understand how to capture value from AI while controlling risk.

As an independent advisory firm, we also partner with consultancies, software businesses, and systems integrators.
