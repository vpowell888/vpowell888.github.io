BeaconGuard

Wi‑Fi spectrum change detection and intruder awareness for wireless environments.

BeaconGuard monitors wireless beacons and spectrum characteristics to detect significant changes, anomalies, and potential intruder activity. It is designed for passive monitoring, rapid alerts, and integration with security workflows.

Request Demo Learn more

Overview

BeaconGuard provides continuous monitoring of Wi‑Fi signals and metadata to identify changes in the radio environment that may indicate interference, rogue APs, or physical intrusions. It combines lightweight agents, signal analysis, and anomaly detection models to surface actionable alerts.

Key Features

  • Passive Wi‑Fi beacon collection (SSID/BSSID/channel/strength)
  • Spectrum change detection and trending
  • Anomaly scoring and alerting for rogue devices
  • Integration hooks for SIEM and incident workflows
  • Lightweight on‑prem or edge deployment

How it works

  1. Agents capture beacon frames and summary spectrum metrics.
  2. Data is aggregated and baseline behaviour models are created.
  3. Real‑time changes are scored against baselines and thresholds.
  4. Alerts are emitted with context and recommended next steps.
