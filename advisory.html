Business Services

CEO-Led AI Transformation & Readiness Assessment

A structured executive diagnostic treating AI as business redesign rather than a technology programme. We assess leadership ownership, operating model readiness, and organisational friction.

Outputs: CEO scorecard, red flags, and a prioritised transformation agenda.

AI Strategy as Business Redesign (P&L & Risk Aligned)

Reposition AI strategy to create durable value and manage enterprise exposure. We align AI initiatives to profit, cost, and risk to move beyond pilots.

Outputs: Value-mapping, dependency & risk assessment, governance and funding recommendations.

Decision Architecture & AI Governance Design

Map critical recurring decisions and define where AI advises, where it decides, and where humans retain override authority.

Outputs: Decision rights, escalation paths, and board-ready AI governance structures aligned to enterprise risk.

Core Workflow AI Embedding & Delivery

Embed AI into revenue or cost-driving workflows to convert experiments into measurable impact — speed, quality, and throughput improvements.

Outputs: End-to-end delivery, KPIs tied to business outcomes, and operationalised workflows.

Board & Executive AI Risk, Measurement & Oversight Programme

Elevate AI from periodic updates to continuous governance: material-risk management, outcome-based metrics, and intervention mechanisms.

Outputs: Board-level playbook, outcome metrics, kill-switches, and accountability frameworks.

Role Redesign & AI Training

Redefine roles, responsibilities and operating model to embed AI into day-to-day work, and deliver targeted training to upskill teams.

Outputs:

  • Role definitions and RACI for AI-enabled processes
  • Training curriculum (technical and non-technical) and learning paths
  • Train-the-trainer materials and onboarding playbooks

Team

Business Intelligence Analyst

Understands business metrics and processes. Turns raw data into reports and dashboards that show what is happening and track KPIs.

Example: Builds Power BI dashboards using SQL queries on Snowflake to show weekly sales, churn, and margin by region.

Data Engineer

Develops data models and engineering patterns for reliable, testable pipelines. Builds and maintains data platforms so data is accurate, timely, and usable.

Example: Creates automated ELT pipelines using Azure Data Factory or Airflow and loads curated tables into Snowflake.

Data Scientist

Builds models of business processes using statistics and machine learning to predict outcomes and recommend actions.

Example: Develops churn prediction models in Python using scikit‑learn or XGBoost, trained on warehouse data and deployed via MLflow.

Solutions Architect

Designs the overall technical solution, bringing together all technical elements, ensuring systems integrate, scale, and meet security and cost requirements.

Example: Designs cloud architecture on AWS or GCP integrating data pipelines, ML models, APIs, and BI tools with IAM and monitoring.
